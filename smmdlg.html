About Me

A Noobies Exploration into Bootstrap


I currently work in the Financial sector, specifically in the private wealth management field. My job is to build portfolio's based on the financial profile of each client. We can build aggressive, income generating, speculative, and defensive portfolios which are dependent on risk factors and age. These portfolios will be diversified by investing in multiple asset classes assets such as SMA's, Mutual Funds, ETF's, Primary and Secondary Offerings, Structured Products, and Alternative Investments. I'm doing this bootcamp to expand my skillset, and to possibly jump into a new career or have this as a freelance job.
